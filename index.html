Everything you want to know

We hear the same great questions all the time. A well-informed client gets better results. Here are the ones we field most often at IYKYK Aesthetics in Hamilton, Ohio.

Ask Us Directly

At IYKYK Aesthetics, Botox is $12 per unit. Most areas use 10 to 40 units. Daxxify is $8 per unit for longer-lasting results. Beverly or Angel will give you an accurate unit estimate based on your anatomy and goals before anything is done.

Botox typically lasts 3 to 4 months with results visible in 3 to 7 days and full effect at 2 weeks. Daxxify can last 6 months or longer for many patients. Consistency between treatments helps extend your results over time.

Yes. Lip filler is one of our most requested services. We use Versa hyaluronic acid filler at $650 per syringe. Most treatments use 0.5 to 1 syringe depending on your goals. Angel Ahr BSN RN specializes in natural-looking lip enhancement.

Daxxify is a neurotoxin like Botox but uses a peptide-based stabilizer that extends its duration — often 6+ months versus Botox's 3 to 4 months. Available at $8 per unit. If frequent appointments are a concern, Daxxify is worth discussing at your consultation.
